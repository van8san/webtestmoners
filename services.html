Short or Long-term Rent

Moners Creative offers both short and long-term rentals of welding chamber habitat. Short-term rentals, ranging from 14-60 days, include temporary repairs such as automatic shutdown systems and pipeline or valve fixes. For clients needing extended use, long-term rentals are available for periods of 1-2 years, ensuring continuous operation and support.

Trained Personnel

Moners Creative provides fully trained personnel for operating the welding chamber habitats. We also offer customized training programs, certifying them to Moners Creative's standards. As the fabricator of the chamber, our certification ensures proper and safe use.

Moners Creative specializes in providing advanced welding chamber habitats designed to address the critical safety concerns of our clients in the oilfield and offshore industries.
