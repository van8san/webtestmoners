What is Welding
Chamber Habitat?

A welding chamber habitat is a pressurized enclosure designed for conducting safe hot work, such as welding, on live hydrocarbon facilities. It is used in oil fields, both offshore and onshore, to prevent flammable gasses from entering the work area, thus reducing the risk of fire and explosions.

How does it work?

The habitat creates a sealed and pressurized environment that prevents flammable gasses from penetrating. It is equipped with an advanced gas detection unit that continuously monitors for hazardous gasses. If any gas is detected, the unit automatically shuts down operations, ensuring maximum safety for workers.

Why do you need Welding Chamber Habitat?

Safety

Our welding chamber habitats allow safe welding on live facilities, preventing flammable gasses from entering the work area and reducing fire and explosion risks.

Cost Saving

By enabling work to continue without shutting down operations, our habitats lead to significant cost savings, avoiding the expenses associated with halting production.

Time Efficiency

The ability to conduct uninterrupted work enhances productivity and reduces downtime, ensuring projects are completed faster and more efficiently.

Why Choose Moners Creative?

Moners Creative offers the first certified Welding Chamber Habitat in Indonesia, meeting Domestic Component Level (TKDN) certification standards. Our habitats feature fireproof panels tested and certified by SUCOFINDO Lab, ensuring compliance with rigorous safety standards (Burning Test Method BS476 Part 7 and Tensile Test Method ASTM D638).

We also offer trained welders, comprehensive training programs, and flexible short and long-term rentals of welding chamber habitats, providing complete support for your operational needs.

Moners Creative specializes in providing advanced welding chamber habitats designed to address the critical safety concerns of our clients in the oilfield and offshore industries.
